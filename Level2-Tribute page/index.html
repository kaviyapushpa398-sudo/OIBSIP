01 — About

About Sundar Pichai

Sundar Pichai is an Indian-born technology executive and the Chief Executive Officer of Google and Alphabet. He is known for his leadership, innovation, and contributions to products used by billions of people around the world.

His journey from a modest background in India to leading one of the world's biggest technology companies has inspired millions of students and professionals across the globe.

02 — Origins

Early Life

Sundar Pichai was born on June 10, 1972, in Madurai, Tamil Nadu, India. He grew up in a middle-class family where his father worked as an electrical engineer for the British firm GEC.

The family lived in a two-room apartment and did not own a car or television for many years — yet young Sundar displayed a remarkable gift for numbers and memory. From an early age, he showed strong curiosity about technology and problem-solving.

His father's work in electrical engineering sparked Sundar's fascination with technology, setting the foundation for an extraordinary future.

07 — Legacy

Why He Is a Role Model

Sundar Pichai's journey shows that dedication, curiosity, and hard work can lead to extraordinary achievements. He grew up without a television, shared a bedroom with his family in a two-room flat, and studied through power cuts — yet rose to lead a company worth over a trillion dollars.

His leadership style — calm, inclusive, and visionary — continues to inspire millions of aspiring engineers and developers around the world. He represents the power of education and the beauty of humble beginnings.
